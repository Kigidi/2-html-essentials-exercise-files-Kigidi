Structural tags:
<nav> is being used for the navigation of the site (the links that take you to other pages)
<article> is being used to wrap up the main content of the page
<section> is being used to divide main content which is already wraped in article to smaller parts - sections
<aside> is being used for the content that is related to the main conted but is of a lesser importance
